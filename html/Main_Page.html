Who am I?

I am an aspiring programmer with a wife and two sons. I currently live in Lehi, Utah; and work for Xactware, also located in Lehi.

My favorite time is when I can spend a quiet evening or adventurous weekend with my family. We all love to hike - especially if there is a waterfall at the end.

When the weather or schedule does not cooperate, I tend to find myself pulled into a puzzle or Lego session.

If the kids are not around, a quick round of video games are usually enjoyed - unless the current book finds itself open.


How did I get here?

I graduated from a small college in Southern Wisconsin called Maranatha Baptist Bible College (now Maranatha Baptist University) in December 2008. My degree was Humanities with an emphasis in History.

I quickly found that the greatest demand in the workforce is developers. While I would not trade the education I received, that was not a skill included.

After graduation the first job I found that could support my new family (then just my wife) was a software company in Utah.

Finding an entry level position was excellent, but I found that more training would be needed to advance.

And with that short explanation, we get to here and now. I am receiving that training, and working toward a full-time development position.
